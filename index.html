Food for a family

Provide nutritious meals to the poor and needy people. Donating groceries can help end the hunger of poor and needy people. They cannot even afford a single time meal for themselves and their family, so donating food will make their stomach fill and stay healthy

Shelter for a family

Help us provide for the basic needs of a family. Throughout the year, SHELTER, Inc. needs a variety of household items, personal care items, and holiday goods to assist families as they transition

Education for a child

Give kids the chance to succeed A good education is a child’s best chance to escape poverty. Giving free education to the poor and needy people help them to grow as individuals and help them lead a better life

Their lives depend on our donation

One of the better ways to help the poor and needy people is to give them a hand up rather than a hand out. Giving them moral support, showing heartfelt humility and respect makes them aware that someone really does care about them and trying to improve their condition.
